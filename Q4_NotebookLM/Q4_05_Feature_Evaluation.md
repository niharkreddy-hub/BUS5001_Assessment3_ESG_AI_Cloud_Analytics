# NotebookLM Feature Evaluation

## Feature 1: AI Research and Source Summarisation

NotebookLM successfully analysed multiple ESG-related sources and generated consolidated summaries.

Strengths:
- Rapid synthesis of information
- Source-grounded responses
- Improved research efficiency

Limitations:
- Potential omission of important details
- Requires source quality validation

---

## Feature 2: AI Report Generation

The AI-generated ESG report demonstrated the ability to create structured content based on uploaded sources.

Strengths:
- Professional structure
- Logical organisation
- Time-saving for researchers

Limitations:
- Requires human fact-checking
- May contain unsupported assumptions

---

## Feature 3: Quiz Generation

NotebookLM automatically generated assessment questions from uploaded ESG resources.

Strengths:
- Useful for revision
- Supports active learning

Limitations:
- Question difficulty may vary
- Requires instructor review

---

## Feature 4: Mind Map Generation

The mind map feature visualised relationships between ESG concepts.

Strengths:
- Improves conceptual understanding
- Useful for teaching and presentations

Limitations:
- Complex topics may be oversimplified

---

## Overall Assessment

NotebookLM is highly suitable for academic and professional ESG research. It improves productivity, supports source-grounded learning and assists with content generation. However, outputs should always be reviewed by humans due to risks of inaccuracies, hallucinations and over-reliance on AI-generated insights.

Recommendation:
Suitable for university adoption with appropriate AI literacy and governance controls.
